Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how TrailFusion AI ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our platform at trailfusionai.com and any associated services, integrations, or APIs.

2. Information We Collect

TrailFusion AI may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you authorize TrailFusion AI to access your social media accounts (e.g., TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, X), we receive authentication tokens and basic profile information (such as username and account ID).
  • Content Data: Images, videos, and text that you upload or create through TrailFusion AI for publishing to social media platforms.
  • Usage Data: Technical information such as device type, browser, and interaction logs to improve our Service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information collected by TrailFusion AI to:

  • Publish content to your authorized social media accounts on your behalf
  • Generate AI-powered captions, descriptions, and marketing copy
  • Process and convert media files (e.g., image-to-video conversion)
  • Improve and maintain the Service
  • Provide customer support

4. Third-Party Services

TrailFusion AI integrates with the following third-party services:

  • TikTok API: To publish video content to your TikTok account
  • Instagram Graph API: To publish images and Reels to your Instagram account
  • YouTube Data API: To upload videos to your YouTube channel
  • X (Twitter) API: To post tweets with media to your X account
  • AI Services: To generate content and analyze images

Your use of these services through TrailFusion AI is also subject to their respective privacy policies.

5. Data Storage and Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. Authentication tokens are stored securely and are never shared with unauthorized parties. Content data is processed for publishing and is not retained longer than necessary.

6. Data Sharing

TrailFusion AI does not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share data with third-party platforms as necessary to provide the Service (e.g., publishing content to your social media accounts).

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data held by TrailFusion AI
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke authorization to access your social media accounts at any time
  • Restrict data processing

8. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the Service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

TrailFusion AI is not intended for use by children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of TrailFusion AI after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Contact Us

For questions about this Privacy Policy or to exercise your data rights, contact us at trailfusionai@gmail.com.
