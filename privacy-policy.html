Privacy Policy for TrailFusion AI

Last Updated: April 22, 2026 / 最終更新日: 2026年4月22日

This Privacy Policy describes how TrailFusion AI ("we", "us", "our", or "the App") collects, uses, discloses, and safeguards your information when you use our application and the website located at https://trailfusionai.com. By using TrailFusion AI, you consent to the data practices described in this policy.

本プライバシーポリシーは、TrailFusion AI （以下「本アプリ」「当方」）が、ユーザーによる本アプリおよび https://trailfusionai.com の利用にあたって、どのような情報を収集・利用・保管・共有するかを定めるものです。

1. Information TrailFusion AI Collects

TrailFusion AI may collect the following categories of information depending on the features you use:

  • Account Identifiers: Your username, user ID, display name, and profile picture from the connected social platform (X/Twitter, TikTok, Instagram, YouTube).
  • Content You Upload: Images, videos, captions, titles, tags, and any metadata you explicitly submit to TrailFusion AI for publishing.
  • Authentication Tokens: OAuth access tokens and refresh tokens required to post on your behalf. Tokens are stored securely and are never shared with third parties.
  • Operational Logs: Timestamps, endpoint paths, and status codes of API calls made by TrailFusion AI, for debugging and abuse prevention only.

2. How TrailFusion AI Uses Information

TrailFusion AI uses information solely to:

  • Authenticate you to the social platforms you connect.
  • Publish the content you explicitly authorize (images, videos, captions).
  • Return the result (success/failure, post URL) back to you.
  • Improve stability and debug failures.

TrailFusion AI does not use your information for advertising, profiling, or sale to third parties.

3. TikTok API Usage (Content Posting API)

When you connect your TikTok account, TrailFusion AI requests the following scopes:

  • user.info.basic — to identify your TikTok account.
  • video.upload — to upload videos that you authorize for publishing.
  • video.publish — to publish those videos on your TikTok profile.

TrailFusion AI complies with the TikTok Developer Terms of Service and the TikTok Community Guidelines. Data retrieved from the TikTok API is used exclusively to complete the posting flow you initiate and is not stored beyond the session required to complete that flow.

4. X (Twitter), Instagram, and YouTube API Usage

TrailFusion AI also integrates with X (Twitter) API v2, Instagram Graph API, and YouTube Data API v3 under the respective platforms' developer agreements. The data accessed is limited to what is strictly needed to publish the content you authorize.

5. Data Retention

TrailFusion AI retains authentication tokens only as long as needed to deliver the service. You may revoke TrailFusion AI's access at any time from the settings of each social platform, after which the tokens are invalidated. Operational logs are retained for up to 30 days for abuse prevention, then automatically deleted.

6. Data Sharing

TrailFusion AI does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by law or to comply with the API terms of the connected platforms.

7. Security

TrailFusion AI stores credentials in encrypted environment variables and uses HTTPS for all API calls. Despite these safeguards, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data TrailFusion AI holds about you.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Revoke TrailFusion AI's access at any time.

9. Children's Privacy

TrailFusion AI is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

TrailFusion AI may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
